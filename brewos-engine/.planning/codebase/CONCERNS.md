## Performance Concerns

- **Strict ODE tolerances** — `rtol=1e-8` is unnecessarily tight for brew simulation accuracy; increases solve time significantly
- **No simulation caching** — Identical simulation parameters re-run from scratch every time
- **Oversized evaluation grid** — 900-point time-series evaluation grid; likely more than needed for most use cases
